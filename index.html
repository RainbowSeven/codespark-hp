Our Story

CodeSpark was born out of the desire to see Africa rise to the top of technological evolution and involvement in economical growth. Imagine what you could have achieved if eight(8) years ago, you knew what you know now.

Our Mission

Every child male/female should be exposed to technology at an early age.

Our Activity

In order to sustain the spark, we engage in the following activities:

  • We train teachers/volunteers
  • We setup creative coding workshops for children.
  • We organize tech competitions.

Our Tools

With our mastery of this easy to use tools developed by MIT, any kid can create anything imaginable.

Scratch

With Scratch, you can program your own interactive stories, games, and animations and share your creations with others in the online community.

AppInventor

MIT App Inventor is a blocks-based programming tool that allows everyone, even novices, to start programming and build fully functional apps for Android devices.
